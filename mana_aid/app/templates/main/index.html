How It Works

Simple steps to make a big difference

1. Post Surplus Food

Donors post available surplus food with details and pickup information.

2. Browse & Request

Recipients browse available food and make requests for what they need.

3. Coordinate Pickup

Volunteers help coordinate pickup and delivery between donors and recipients.

4. Share & Connect

Food reaches those in need, reducing waste and building community connections.
